How It Works

Your Path to Pain-Free Movement

Our approach is systematic, patient-centered, and built around long-term outcomes. No quick fixes.

01
Consultation

We listen carefully to your history, symptoms, and goals. Understanding you is step one.

02
Assessment

A thorough biomechanical evaluation: gait analysis, structural assessment, and imaging when needed.

03
Treatment Plan

A personalized plan starting with the most conservative options first. Custom orthotics, physical therapy guidance, or targeted intervention.

04
Follow-Through

Ongoing monitoring and adjustment. We're invested in your long-term outcome, not just the next appointment.

A Custom Orthotic System Unlike Anything Else

Dr. Santopietro's patented EvenKeel Orthotic System was decades in the making. It's a precisely engineered solution that addresses the biomechanical root causes of foot and body pain.

Patented Technology

The only orthotic system of its kind, developed and refined over 50 years of clinical practice and patient outcomes.

Precision-Crafted Per Patient

Every orthotic is built from a photo analysis of your feet and a gait video, using proprietary technology we developed to capture exactly what each patient needs.

Whole-Body Impact

Correcting foot mechanics can resolve pain in the knees, hips, and lower back, not just the feet.

Built for Activity

Designed to work in everyday shoes, athletic footwear, and specialized gear. Supportive, without limiting your lifestyle.

Meet the Doctor

50 Years of Putting Patients First

D.P.M. Certified Sports Medicine Custom Orthotics Pediatric Podiatry

Dr. Frank Santopietro established his podiatry practice in Brookline in 1974. Over five decades, he has developed a deep expertise in sports medicine, biomechanics, and custom orthotic fabrication, including his own patented EvenKeel Orthotic System.

His philosophy is rooted in conservative care: understanding the whole patient, exhausting non-surgical options first, and building solutions that last. That approach has earned him the trust of thousands of patients, from weekend warriors to pediatric patients and everyone in between.
