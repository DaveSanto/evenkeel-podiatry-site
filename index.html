How It Works

Your Path to Pain-Free Movement

Our approach is systematic, patient-centered, and built around long-term outcomes — not quick fixes.

01
Consultation

We listen carefully to your history, symptoms, and goals. No rushed appointments — understanding you is step one.

02
Assessment

A thorough biomechanical evaluation — gait analysis, structural assessment, and imaging when needed.

03
Treatment Plan

A personalized plan starting with the most conservative options first. Custom orthotics, physical therapy guidance, or targeted intervention.

04
Follow-Through

Ongoing monitoring and adjustment. We're invested in your long-term outcome — not just your next appointment.

Meet the Doctor

50 Years of Putting Patients First

D.P.M. Certified Sports Medicine Custom Orthotics Pediatric Podiatry

Dr. Frank Santopietro established his podiatry practice in Brookline in 1974. Over five decades, he has developed a deep expertise in sports medicine, biomechanics, and custom orthotic fabrication — including his own patented EvenKeel Orthotic System.

His philosophy is rooted in conservative care: understanding the whole patient, exhausting non-surgical options first, and building solutions that last. That approach has earned him the trust of thousands of patients — from weekend warriors to pediatric patients and everyone in between.
